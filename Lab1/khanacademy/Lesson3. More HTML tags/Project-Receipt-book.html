Recipe #1

  • Time: 3 hrs 15 mins
  • Serves: 12
2 tablespoons white sugar
Ingredients Quantity
Sweet Italian sausage 1 pound
Lean ground beef ¾ pound
minced onion ½ cup pound
crushed garlic 2 cloves
crushed tomatoes 1 (28 ounce)
canned tomato sauce 2 (6.5 ounce)
water ½ cup
white sugar 2 tablespoons
chopped fresh parsley, divided 4 tablespoons
dried basil leaves 1 ½ teaspoons
salt, divided, or to taste 1 ½ teaspoons
Italian seasoning 1 teaspoon
fennel seeds ½ teaspoon
ground black pepper ¼ teaspoon
lasagna noodles 12
ricotta cheese 16 ounces
egg 1
grated Parmesan cheese ¾ cup


Step 1: Make the meat sauce.

Step 2:Cook the noodles.

Step 3:Make the ricotta mixture.

Step 4:Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.

Step 5:Cover with foil and bake.

Step 6:Let the lasagna rest before serving.
